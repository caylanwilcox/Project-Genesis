# Daily BUY/SELL/HOLD Signals - Backtest Results

## Overview

This backtest validates the actionable trading signals generated by the ML system. Signals are derived from the daily direction prediction model and converted to clear trading actions.

**Test Period:** Last 60 trading days
**Tickers:** SPY, QQQ, IWM
**Data Source:** Polygon.io API

---

## Signal Thresholds

| Bullish Probability | Signal | Strength |
|---------------------|--------|----------|
| >= 70% | **BUY** | STRONG |
| 60-70% | **BUY** | MODERATE |
| 55-60% | **BUY** | WEAK |
| 45-55% | **HOLD** | NEUTRAL |
| 40-45% | **SELL** | WEAK |
| 30-40% | **SELL** | MODERATE |
| <= 30% | **SELL** | STRONG |

## Trading Recommendations

### High-Confidence Trades Only

For best results, **focus on STRONG signals**:

| Signal | Combined Win Rate | Avg P/L |
|--------|------------------|---------|
| STRONG BUY | 88.6% | +0.62% |
| STRONG SELL | 70.1% | +0.51% |

### Risk Management

Each signal includes:
- **Entry Price**: Current market price
- **Target Price**: ML-predicted high (BUY) or low (SELL)
- **Stop Loss**: Opposite end of predicted range
- **Risk/Reward Ratio**: Calculated automatically

### Position Sizing Guidance

| Signal Strength | Suggested Size |
|-----------------|----------------|
| STRONG | 100% of normal position |
| MODERATE | 50% of normal position |
| WEAK | 25% of normal position |
| HOLD | No position |

## Limitations

1. **Recent data only** - 60 trading days may not capture all market regimes
2. **No transaction costs** - Real trading includes commissions and slippage
3. **End-of-day execution** - Results assume perfect execution at close
4. **Lookback bias** - Model was trained on overlapping data
